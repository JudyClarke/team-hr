Judy Clarke

Judy Clarke has over 20 years of professional experience in consulting and facilitation.  She has developed long-term relationships with her clients, helping them develop strategies for success and problem solving.  She’s known as a “straight shooter” who can get to the heart of issues and build collaboration, helping individuals and diverse groups see new possibilities..

Judy focuses on developing and implementing organizational change strategies for both large and small organizations nationwide – at the company, team, and individual levels.  A vibrant, caring, and motivating person, she offers exceptional skills in executive coaching and in all areas of communication, including conflict resolution.  Her belief in win-win solutions has resulted in lasting, organization-wide improvements

Judy earned her B.S. and M.Ed. in Counseling from Lewis and Clark College.  She is certified as a Myers-Briggs facilitator.
