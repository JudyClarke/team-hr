WHO WE ARE

TeamHR, LLC is a values-driven human resources and organizational development firm dedicated to providing comprehensive solutions to each client’s unique needs.

Judy Clarke and her team of Associates use their extensive experience and diverse skills to assist their clients to achieve their business goals.

HOW WE THINK

Our Mission:

To team with organizational leaders to facilitate excellence through innovative human resources and organizational development practices.

Our Values:

  • To consistently exceed client expectations.
  • To model courage, integrity, and honesty.
  • To communicate with respect.
  • To honor diversity.
  • To create a healthy, fun and balanced work environment

To us, a true team functions as a single unit, through collaboration and trust.  It makes the difficult do-able.  It prepares for the unexpected. 

TeamHR, LLC is a women-owned business and is certified as a Woman Business Enterprise (WBE) and an Disadvantaged Business Enterprise (DBE) by the State of Oregon.
